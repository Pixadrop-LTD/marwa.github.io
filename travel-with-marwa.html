YOUR SAFARI EXPERIENCE

A Day on Safari with Marwa

Immerse yourself in the rhythm of the African wilderness with our carefully curated daily itinerary, blending adventure, relaxation, and authentic cultural experiences.

05:30 AM

Morning Game Drive

Awaken to the African dawn as we set out to witness nature's most magical moments. The cool morning air carries the sounds of the bush coming to life.

Experience Includes

  • Sunrise photography opportunities
  • Wildlife tracking with expert guides
  • Complimentary coffee and pastries
09:00 AM

Bush Breakfast

Savor a sumptuous breakfast in a scenic bush setting, with the sounds of nature as your soundtrack and the possibility of wildlife sightings.

Meal Highlights

  • Fresh tropical fruits and pastries
  • Made-to-order omelets and local specialties
  • Freshly brewed coffee and selection of teas
04:00 PM

Afternoon Safari

As the day cools, we venture back into the wilderness when predators become more active. The golden light creates perfect conditions for photography and wildlife viewing.

Safari Highlights

  • Prime wildlife viewing during golden hour
  • Sundowner drinks at a scenic viewpoint
  • Expert tracking of big cats and other predators
07:30 PM

Gourmet Dinner

Savor a multi-course dinner under the stars, sharing stories of the day's adventures and listening to the sounds of the African night.

Dining Experience

  • Multi-course fine dining under the stars
  • Local and international cuisine with African flavors
  • Selection of fine wines and signature cocktails
