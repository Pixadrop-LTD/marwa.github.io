When to Visit East Africa

Best Times to Visit

Peak Season

July to October for the Great Migration and optimal wildlife viewing.

Shoulder Season

January to March for bird watching and newborn animals.

Wildlife Viewing

Great Migration July-Oct
Big Cats Year-round
Bird Watching Nov-Apr

Seasonal Highlights

Dry Season

June - October

  • Ideal for wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources
  • Clear skies and minimal rainfall for excellent game drives
  • Great Migration in the Maasai Mara (July-October)

Green Season

November - May

  • Lush landscapes and beautiful scenery with fewer tourists
  • Bird watching at its peak with migratory species present
  • Lower rates and more availability at lodges and camps
